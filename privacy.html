Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

MUSE Social Publisher ("Service", "we", "us", "our") is a social media management platform that helps toy brands and retailers publish content to social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we may collect:

  • Authentication Data: OAuth tokens provided by social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook) to authorize content publishing on your behalf.
  • Profile Information: Basic profile information (display name, username, profile URL) from connected social media accounts, solely to identify the authenticated user and display account information within the Service.
  • Content Data: Product images and text content that you choose to publish through the Service.
  • Usage Data: Basic usage metrics such as publish timestamps and post performance data retrieved from platform APIs.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your access to social media platform APIs
  • Publish content to your authorized social media accounts
  • Display your account information and post analytics within the Service
  • Maintain, improve, and secure the Service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely with encrypted storage and restricted file permissions. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party platforms:

  • TikTok (Content Posting API, Login Kit)
  • Instagram (Graph API)
  • Facebook (Pages API)

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing data they collect. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies linked above.

6. Data Retention

We retain authentication tokens only as long as necessary to provide the Service. Tokens are automatically refreshed as needed and old tokens are securely overwritten. You may revoke access at any time through the respective social media platform's settings or by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke social media platform authorizations at any time
  • Opt out of the Service entirely
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Our Service operates from Dubai, United Arab Emirates. If you access the Service from outside the UAE, your information may be transferred to and processed in the UAE. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
