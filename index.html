How It Works

1

Connect Your TikTok Account

Click "Connect TikTok" and authorize MUSE Social Publisher through TikTok's official OAuth flow. We request only the permissions needed to publish content on your behalf.

2

Upload Your Product Content

Add product images, write captions, and choose hashtags. Our platform optimizes your content for each social media platform's requirements.

3

Publish to TikTok

Hit publish and your product content goes live on your brand's TikTok profile. The Content Posting API handles the upload and posting automatically.

4

Track Performance

Monitor how your posts perform across platforms. Use insights to refine your content strategy and grow your toy brand's social presence.
