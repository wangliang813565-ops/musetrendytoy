Social Media Content Publisher

About

MUSE Social Publisher is a web-based social media management tool for MUSE Trendy Toys, a toy retail brand based in Dubai. It helps manage and publish product content across multiple social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

How It Works

Our tool automates the process of publishing product photos and descriptions to social media. The brand owner authenticates via OAuth and the system handles content formatting and posting through official platform APIs.
